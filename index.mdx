<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-3xl font-bold text-gray-900 dark:text-white mb-4">Quick start</h2>
  <p className="text-base text-gray-600 dark:text-gray-400 mb-8">Get up and running with Poge in three simple steps</p>
  
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Open Poge">
      Visit [poge.dev](https://poge.dev) in your browser or deploy your own instance on Vercel. No installation required.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Connect your database">
      Click **Add Server** and enter your PostgreSQL connection details:
      
      ```bash
      Host: your-database-host.com
      Port: 5432
      Database: your_database
      Username: your_username
      Password: your_password
      ```
      
      <Note>All connection details are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to external servers.</Note>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Start exploring">
      Browse tables, run SQL queries, and manage your data through the intuitive interface. Your query history and notes are automatically saved.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
